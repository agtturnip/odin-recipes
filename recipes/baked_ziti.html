Classic Baked Ziti Recipe



A comforting pasta bake with ziti noodles, ricotta cheese, marinara sauce, and melted mozzarella.

Baked Ziti is a classic, cheesy, and comforting pasta dish made with layers of ziti pasta, creamy ricotta, rich marinara sauce, and plenty of melted mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or meal prepping!


Ingredients

  • For the Pasta:
    • 1 lb ziti pasta
    • Salt (for boiling water)

  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp black pepper

  • For the Cheese Mixture:
    • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

    Instructions

    1. Cook the Pasta
      1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook ziti until al dente according to package instructions.
      2. Drain and set aside.

    2. Prepare the Sauce
      1. In a large skillet, cook ground meat over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
      2. Add diced onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes. Stir in minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
      3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.

    3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
      1. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, and 1 cup of mozzarella until well combined.

    4. Assemble and Bake
      1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
      2. In a greased 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a layer of sauce.
      3. Add half of the cooked ziti, then spread half of the cheese mixture over it.
      4. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce on top.
      5. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella over the dish.
      6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
      7. Let sit for 10 minutes before serving.
