Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe



Bell peppers filled with a savory mixture of ground meat, rice, and tomatoes, baked to perfection.

Stuffed Bell Peppers are a delicious and nutritious meal made with tender bell peppers packed with a hearty mixture of ground meat, rice, tomatoes, and seasonings, then baked until perfectly tender and flavorful.


Ingredients

  • For the Peppers:
    • 4 large bell peppers (any color), tops removed, seeds removed

  • For the Filling:
    • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
    • 1 small onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup cooked rice
    • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ½ tsp black pepper
    • ½ cup shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese (for topping)

    Instructions

    1. Prepare the Peppers
      1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
      2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
      3. Place the peppers upright in a baking dish.

    2. Make the Filling
      1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground meat until browned. Drain excess fat.
      2. Add onion and garlic, cooking until softened, about 3 minutes.
      3. Stir in cooked rice, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
      4. Cook for 5 more minutes, then remove from heat.

    3. Stuff and Bake
      1. Spoon the filling evenly into the hollowed-out bell peppers.
      2. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
      3. Remove foil, sprinkle cheese on top, and bake for another 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted.
      4. Serve warm and enjoy!
